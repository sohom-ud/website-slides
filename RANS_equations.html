Incompressible Navier-Stokes equations

$\frac{\partial u_i}{\partial t} + u_j\frac{\partial}{\partial x_j}u_i = -\frac{1}{\rho}\frac{\partial p}{\partial x_i} + \nu\frac{\partial^2 u_i}{\partial x_j\partial x_j}$



Incompressibility

$\frac{\partial u_i}{\partial x_i} = 0$


Reynolds decomposition

$u_i = \overline{u_i}+u_i',\quad p = \overline{p}+p'$
